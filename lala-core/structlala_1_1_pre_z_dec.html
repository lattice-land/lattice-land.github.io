Detailed Description

template<class VT>
struct lala::PreZDec< VT >

PreZDec is a pre-abstract universe \( \langle \{\infty, \ldots, 2, 1, 0, -1, -2, \ldots, -\infty\}, \leq \rangle \) totally ordered by the reversed natural arithmetic comparison operator. It is used to represent constraints of the form \( x \leq k \) where \( k \) is an integer.
